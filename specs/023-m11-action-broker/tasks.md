- **Exactly-once dispatch requires `FOR UPDATE SKIP LOCKED` on the claim plus a terminal status transition** (M1/M9/M10 discipline): the dispatcher must check that the claimed row's status is still in the dispatchable set after locking (a row may transition between the LISTEN notify and the claim). - **GitHub comment-create is NOT natively idempotent** (plan Phase 0 note): there is no provider-side idempotency key. Exactly-once is enforced entirely Garrison-side by the claim + terminal-status transition. This is why auto-retry (FR-022/D12) is forbidden — a retry of an in-doubt POST could double-post.
- **Vault PAT is a `vault.SecretValue`; `.Zero()` must be called after use** (M2.3 vaultlog discipline): the dispatcher passes the PAT as a `string` only to the HTTP header, not to any logger. The `TestPostCommentNeverLogsPAT` test pins this.
